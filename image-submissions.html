Step 1 · Upload Your Visual Asset

All raw image resources must reside directly within our global cloud database path inside the GitHub engine. You will need to:

  • Use the Upload Files button in the /images root folder link below.
  • Ensure your graphic asset is compressed to web formats (.jpg, .jpeg, or .png).
  • Name your file cleanly: artwork-title_your-name.jpg (e.g., neon-reflections_meenz.jpg).
Optimization Tip: Keep image dimensions below 2000px on the longest edge to ensure high resolution across standard screens while keeping layouts lightweight.

Step 2 · Map Your Frontmatter Pointer

After uploading your image, create an article entry file under the /articles directory to index your graphic. Copy this snippet to reference your uploaded asset asset file location correctly:

title: "Title of Your Artwork"
author: "Your Full Name"

category: "Visual Arts" # Options: Visual Arts | Photography | Comic Strips
