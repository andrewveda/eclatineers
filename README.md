VEC Eclatineers was created with the objective of providing a space to foster creativity. We aim at multifaceted development of our students. From writing, sketching, poetry to digital art we give our students a platform to showcase their brilliance. Our magazine thus intends on aiding our students in their process of self-exploration. Here we offer a glimpse into the magic that erupts when technology meets arts. The term 'Eclatineers' was coined by blending the words "eclat" and "engineers" which comes to mean a brilliant, flamboyant and an exuberant display by engineers and that defines our objective. We therefore strive towards the holistic development of our students.
